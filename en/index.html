Why Winter Silence

Christmas was always meant to be a season of quieting down. Today it's often the opposite — shopping rushes, deadlines, expectations. Yoga calls us back to what truly matters. And when a sound bowl is struck, its vibration moves through us: loosening old tension, opening what has become tight.
