Activities in Detail

From energizing flows to deep sound relaxation — here's how your program comes together.

Movement

Yoga Flows

From the gentle "Slow Flow Morning Yoga" to the energizing "Energizing Yoga" session to "Winter Wake-Up Yoga" on departure day — the flows are the active backbone of the program, led by Chris & Vlad.

  • Suitable for all levels, no prior experience needed
  • Typically more active in the morning, calmer in the evening
  • No competition — the practice adapts to the group

Regeneration

Yin & Restorative Yoga

The quiet counterpart to the active flows: in "Yin Yoga & Sound Bath" on arrival day and the "Restorative Yoga & Sound Bath Experience" on Saturday, poses are held long and passively — supported by sound.

  • Gentle stretching instead of exertion
  • Paired with singing bowl accompaniment
  • Ideal for settling in after travel

Chris's Signature

Sound Healing

Christian's specialty: 1-on-1 and group sound healing with Tibetan singing bowls and gongs. Group sessions are fixed parts of the program (including the "Sleep Easy" candlelight ritual on arrival day).

  • Group sound healing included in the program
  • Optional: 1-on-1 or couple's session, Fri & Sat at 12:30 (+€50, by appointment)

Relaxation

Spa & Sauna

During free time, the Monasterium Spa with sauna and relaxation area is open to you — the perfect counterbalance between program items.

Indulgence

Culinary

A large breakfast buffet and 3-course dinner at Restaurant Culinaro are included in the price — water at the table included. The Mönchsküche provides a special setting on select evenings.

Extra

Christmas Market & Mystery Event

On Friday and Saturday there's time for an optional visit to the Christmas market right by the hotel. And on Friday evening, the free Mystery Event awaits — what exactly happens is deliberately kept a surprise.
